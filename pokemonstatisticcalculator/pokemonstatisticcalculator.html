pokemonstatisticcalculator
Pokemon is a Japanese media franchise encompassing video games, animated series and films, a trading card
game, and other related media. This tool can be utlised to calculate the statistics of any pokemon
throughout the various generations that were released.

equations
GEN I/II HP: (((Base + IV) * 2 + (SQRT(EV) / 4) * Level) / 100) + Level + 10
GEN I/II OtherStat: (((Base + IV) * 2 + (SQRT(EV) / 4) * Level) / 100) + 5

GEN III+ HP: ((2 * Base + IV + (SQRT(EV) / 4) * Level) / 100) + Level + 10
GEN III+ OtherStat: (((2 * Base + IV + (SQRT(EV) / 4) * Level) / 100) + 5) x Nature
