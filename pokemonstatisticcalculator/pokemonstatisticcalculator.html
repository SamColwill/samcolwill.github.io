pokemon statistic calculator
Pokémon is a Japanese media franchise that includes video games, animated series and films, a trading card
game, and more. This tool is intended to be used for calculating the statistics of the over 1000 Pokémon
that can be found in the core series of video games, from Pokémon Red released 1996 to Pokémon Scarlet
in 2022.

It is particularly useful for those who utlise emulators to play Pokémon games as they are required to
simulate the trading of Pokémon by editing Pokémon save files, part of this process is accurately
determining a Pokémon's evolved statistics following an artifical trade evolution.
